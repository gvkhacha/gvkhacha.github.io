Blogging

I was recently encouraged by a close friend of mine to begin blogging as a method for documenting and sharing my thoughts and passions. As I spend much of my time with coursework, I expect some of the categories to be based on concepts observed from class. I found that many courses, as expected with large classes and 10-week periods, focus more on the technicalities and semantics rather than the practical applications. I feel as though my coursework is not complete without either an application or observation of practical and real world experiences; and document this journey through these posts.
